My Past Works

Here is a little gallery of some of my works I have done for college assignments. Hover you mouse cursor over a work piece to learn a bit about it.

Redesigned Movie Poster

For this assignment, the goal was to put a creative spin on what we thought were boring movie posters.

I was inspired by The Great Wave off Kanagawa and went for a cel shaded look.

Found Letter Photography

This is a found letter photography that I created for my Typography class.

Found letter photography is finding abstract letters in everyday objects/nature, and photographing them to create a collection.

Music Festival Poster

For this assignment, we were tasked with creating an original poster for a music festival.

I decided to create a poster for a country music show. I based the design of the poster on a stick of Old Spice deodorant.
